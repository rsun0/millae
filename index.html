Our Focus

Pure research.

Most companies split their attention between research and product, or crowd into the same handful of trendy approaches. Some academic labs do the same. We do neither. Millae is a research effort focused purely on fundamental questions — deliberately working the under-invested, unfashionable corners of AI research, where we believe the highest-leverage progress is still waiting to be found.

01

Our background

We build on what we learned from researching reinforcement learning and LLMs in the past. We also draw wisdom and inspiration from our past work experience (software engineering), non-profit leadership experience, therapy, as well as Buddhism, Taoism, etc.

02

Current interests

We're currently selecting our next research topics. We're exploring:

  • How can we make models more creative?
  • How can we give models more long-term memory, like humans have? (using compression?)
  • How can we utilize insights from other domains, like psychology, neuroscience, anthropology, economics, etc?
How We Work

We don't rush.

Good research takes time, and we're not interested in a publication treadmill to prove otherwise. We work at the pace the problem actually requires, and we'd rather walk in the right direction than risk sprinting in the wrong direction.

01

Slow science

We're philosophically aligned with Yoshua Bengio's call to rethink the pace of ML publication and the Slow Science manifesto. A tree that grows quickly may catch the eye, but it can be easily shaken when the wind comes. The more we rush to achieve, the more easily life is consumed; a life patiently built endures.

02

Committed to the open-source community

We believe the field goes further in the long run through open collaboration, rather than siloed proprietary efforts. We see ourselves as just one team contributing to the broader collective effort to reach general intelligence. If your lab is interested in collaborating with us, please email us.
