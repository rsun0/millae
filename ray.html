Ray Sun

Founding Researcher, Millae

Seoul, South Korea

ray@millae.ai LinkedIn

Ray Sun is a founding researcher at Millae, where he leads the team's research program toward human-level general intelligence. Ray started Millae with a simple premise: that the fastest way to make progress on general intelligence is to build a small, focused research team with no other mandate.

Ray's research background is in reinforcement learning. His current work sits at the intersection of mechanistic interpretability, meta-learning — particularly meta-reinforcement learning — and insights drawn from neuroscience and other disciplines outside machine learning.

Based in Seoul, Ray is building Millae's founding research culture from first principles — recruiting a small number of cofounder researchers to join him at the ground floor.
