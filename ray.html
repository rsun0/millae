ray@millae.ai LinkedIn CV

Ray Sun is a founding researcher at Millae, where he leads the team's research program toward human-level general intelligence. Ray started Millae with a simple premise: that the best way to make progress on general intelligence is to build a small, focused research team with no other mandate.

Ray's research background is in reinforcement learning and cybersecurity. He was previously a senior software engineer at Meta. He is considering mechanistic interpretability, long-term memory/compression, and metacognition for his next research topic.
